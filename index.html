About

For the past six years, the Luther Center for Global Learning (CGL) has been engaged in an on-going project to study student participation in Luther’s extensive study away programs. The project involved identifying data sources and standardizing data collection methods, cleaning data, and merging data collected in the CGL with other Luther data sources. In addition, nationwide data comparisons are made possible through the Institute of International Education’s Open Doors Report.
